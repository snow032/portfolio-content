EVOLVING CONNECTION between affection and ecology

DaQing is a prefecture-level city in the west of Heilongjiang province, People's Republic of China. The city is developed due to discovery of oil. However, Nowadays, with the sharp decline in production and environmental pollution, people living in Daqing have gradually developed complex ecological problem and affection disorder. PRACTICAL FORM-CORRIDOR&VIRTUAL FORM-CONNECTION: solving ecological problem and affective disorder. Nowadays, with the sharp decline in production and environmental pollution, people living in Daqing have gradually developed complex and contradictory emotions towards the city they used to be proud of. According site's axis and pattern, the possible junction core is determined to maximumly increase connection between local residents and nature.


01 Site pattern: The design obtains the pattern of the base through route and land patches. 02 Basic geometry: The design adjusts the pattern boundary to form the basic geometry. 03 dilution & drainage: By generating a hexagonal ecological purification terrain, the design produces a drainage effect on water. 04 Excavation & accumulation: By digging the surface salinized soil, the design purifies soil by composting. 05 Cohesion & axis: According to the site axis, the design rotate, stretch, and scale each ecological site to produce dynamic changes and a better onflow effect. 06 Refinement & decoration: According to the axis characteristics, the design connect the protrusions to create a connection between man and nature.

In order to ensure rain infiltration, choosing plants who both have a low Leaf absorbability and a canopy rainwater retention ability. Abundant height difference in functional areas not only use Landfill soil to rebuild local ecology, but also make site more interesting to release people's affective disorder.
